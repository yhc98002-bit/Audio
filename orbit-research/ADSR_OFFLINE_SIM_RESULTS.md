## Scope and honesty

- **OFFLINE analysis on the cached BoN-8 pool only.** No ADSR/EVPD/restart real generation is produced here.
- **EVPD / vocal-presence / type-match axis is DEFERRED/PLANNED** (no measured labels). ADSR is run **WITHOUT** the type-match branch and labeled **ADSR-noEVPD (early-quality + late-axis-defer only)**.
- **sigma {0.5, 0.3} are NOT in this cached pool** (only 0.9/0.8/0.7/final). Those rows come from a parallel GPU re-collection.
- **Late-axis-risk score is a within-prompt early-semantic/lyric uncertainty PROXY** (no `LATE_AXIS_RISK` model artifact exists in this pool).
- **CENTRAL RISK / re-selection collapse:** on a *fixed* 8-candidate pool a "restart" cannot draw a genuinely new seed, so every restart method here partially reduces to **re-SELECTION** among the cached 8. The real test is an **ONLINE pilot with true new seeds** (NOT yet run). Full ADSR (with EVPD type-match) and the online pilot are **NOT yet run**.
- **Lyric rule:** `lyric_intelligibility` headline uses the **vocal_scorable subset only (vocal AND en), n=282 prompts.** Instrumental (final_lyric=1.0 sentinel) and non-EN are excluded from all lyric headlines.

## Compute accounting

- Sampler = 30 steps/candidate. Cached early checkpoints: sigma0.9->step7, sigma0.8->step12, sigma0.7->step16.
- Full candidate = 30 sampler steps (+ decode). Full-BoN-8 reference = 8x30 = 240 step-units (sampler-only; matches dataset `full_bon8_step_units`=240).
- Restarted candidate pays only the steps to its restart sigma (sunk); BoN-k discards are never started (cost 0); committed candidate pays full.
- `compute_fraction` = method_compute / Full-BoN-8 compute. Headline rows are matched at compute_fraction ~= 0.53.
- Decode modeled as 0 extra units in the headline (sampler-only, reconciles with the 240 reference); a decode-inclusive sensitivity (+3 units on commit) is reported below.

Dataset: 512 prompts x 8 = 4096 candidates. Strata: vocal/instrumental; splits: dev/held_out. Full BoN-8 oracle ceiling (mean pool-best PRIMARY final) = **2.3576**.
