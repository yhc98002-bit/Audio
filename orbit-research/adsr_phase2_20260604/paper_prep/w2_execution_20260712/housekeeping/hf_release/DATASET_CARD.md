# ADSR Evidence Package

This staged dataset documents Axis-Deferred Speculative Restart experiments for
flow-matching music generation. Frozen primary evidence uses ACE-Step v1. Stable
Audio 3 Medium is a separate cross-backbone pilot.

## Included After Approval

- the 4,096-candidate trajectory dataset spine and reproducibility metadata;
- deduplicated Stage 3 and N2 ledgers and figure data;
- a curated retained-audio audit sample identified by release manifests;
- checksums, scoring scripts, audit reports, and instrument metadata; and
- the static evidence demo.

## Limitations

The main categorical constraint is vocal presence versus instrumental request.
Selected difficult-prompt rates are not generic population prevalence. W2
corrected-instrument estimates remain sensitivity analyses unless the signed
human calibration and promotion gate pass. The evidence does not prove absence
of quality loss and does not establish a causal vocal-generation bias.
