# Limitations And Wording Register

## Required limitations

- Corrected instrument promotion is mechanical but not adopted: both W2 signatures are absent.
- A-prime remains blocked because current PI gold has only 43 total Label-A negatives, below the frozen 50-negative judge-validation minimum.
- B-prime non-inferiority was not established; single expert rater, 40% ties, pre-W2 pair selection, and the t4 same-session deviation apply.
- Stable Audio 3 is a bounded pilot, not full second-backbone equivalence.
- Difficult/selected test-set rates are not generic population rates.

## Banned wording

- proved no loss
- no quality loss
- no degradation
- quality preserved
- impossible to retry
- generic population rate from the selected/difficult set
- causal vocal-generation bias

Use `rare / impractical to retry`, instrument-qualified quality wording, and `consistent with a vocal-generation bias`.
